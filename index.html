How to make an omelette

Necessary Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • salt & pepper
  • butter & oil

    • Optional Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • cheese
  • potato
  • spinach
  • Tomato
  • Avocado

    • Steps

    1. Beat the eggs: Take your eggs, crack them into a bowl and begin to beat them lightly with a fork.

    2. Season the eggs: Once you have beaten the eggs, season them with some salt & pepper.

    3. Prep the pan: Get some butter and oil and heat them over a medium-low heat until the butter has melted and is foaming.

    4. Add the eggs:Poor the eggs into the pan and then tilt the pan from side to side to allow the eggs to cover the it completely.

    5. Fill the Omelettte: Now you can fill the omelette with whatever you like.

    6. Fold and Serve:Now it's time to serve you dish, fold the omelette in half and slide it onto a plate the help of a spatula.
